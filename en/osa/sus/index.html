Sustainable Strategy System

The Most Trusted

Sustainable Voyage

Hanwha Ocean set a vision of the “most trusted company enabling sustainable voyage” and selected three directions and seven tasks to achieve the vision.
We also adopted an ESG framework called GO PLUS+ into our ESG vision to reflect the company’s core values. Our major strategic tasks  include climate action, shared growth and coexistence, and establishment of transparent and fair governance.

  • ESG Vision
    The most trusted company enabling sustainable voyage
  • ESG Slogan
    Go Great Ocean,Go Green Builder
  • ESG Framework
    • People
      We put people at the center of everything, communicate, and respect them.
    • Leadership
      We develop and research novel technologies that will shape the new future, and lead innovation.
    • Upgrade
      We constantly inspect, improve, and achieve unrivaled quality.
    • Safety
      We inspect thoroughly and act promptly to protect our priority and safety.
    • Plus
      We constantly strive for a sustainable ocean and humanity.
  • Directions & ESG Tasks
    • Climate Action through the Advancement of Green and Digital Technologies
      Advancement of environmental management Leading eco-friendly shipbuilding and marine technology
    • Shared Growth and Coexistence through Communication and Trust
      Coexistence with suppliers and local communities Ensuring a safe and pleasant workplace Strengthening human rights management
    • Establishment of Transparent and Fair Governance
      Advancement of ESG management Reinforcement of the compliance system
